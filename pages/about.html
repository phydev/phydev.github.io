Ollantaytambo Sanctuary, Peru

I am an early career researcher in the fields of Computational Biology and Bioinformatics. I obtained my PhD in Physics with focus on Computational Physics at the University of Coimbra (Portugal) in 2020. I have particularly interest in inherent biological mechanisms of tumour dynamics, such as the growing of new blood vessels and cellular migration.

I work in the development of mathematical models to describe biological systems. My main guideline when constructing a model is simplicity while preserving the essence of the biological phenomenon. The goal is always to learn something new about the system and answer at least one question.
